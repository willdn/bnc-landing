Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Build & Conquer ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by BNC Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile game and services ("the Game").

By creating an account or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address and the username you choose. We store your password in hashed form and never in plain text.

Game Data

We collect and store all game state associated with your account: buildings, army composition, resources, research progress, combat history, alliance membership, and in-game transactions.

Technical Data

We collect your IP address for fraud prevention and account security purposes. We may collect device type and operating system to provide technical support and optimize game performance.

Communications

If you contact our support team, we retain those communications to resolve your inquiry and improve our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your game account
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Game
  • To process in-app purchases and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • To send game-related notifications (incoming attacks, market activity, alliance events)
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and investigate violations
  • To respond to support requests
  • To analyze aggregate gameplay patterns and improve game balance

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the Game (hosting, payment processing, push notifications). These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public from harm.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention records).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@buildnconquer.com.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

The Game uses browser local storage to remember your language preference and session token. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-game notification. Continued use of the Game after the effective date of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at info@buildnconquer.com.
